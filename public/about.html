K. M Janaka gunarathna

Founder & Visionary, AICraft

AICraft is a creative AI-powered video production platform founded by K. M Janaka gunarathna, a final-year undergraduate student at the University of Kelaniya, currently pursuing a BA (Hons) in Economics.

With a strong academic background in Economics, the founder brings a unique combination of analytical thinking and creative innovation to the digital content industry. This allows AICraft to not only focus on creativity but also understand market needs, business growth, and effective digital communication.

AICraft was established with the vision of combining technology and creativity to create new business opportunities in the digital world. The motivation behind this platform is to provide innovative AI-based video solutions that support businesses, education, and personal branding in a more modern and efficient way.

Through AI-powered tools and creative storytelling, AICraft aims to transform ideas into high-quality visual content that delivers impact and value. The platform is built on the belief that digital creativity can open doors to financial opportunities and empower individuals and businesses alike.
